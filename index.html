Our Company


PT Enerwise Solusi Indonesia is a company that specializes in providing smart, simple, and user-friendly solutions for various applications of electrical and automation monitoring and control systems. The company aims to simplify industrial solutions to a more user-friendly product by "finding simplicity".

The company was established in 2017 and is based in Tangerang, Banten, Indonesia. The company's mission is to be the most preferred electrical and automation partner.
